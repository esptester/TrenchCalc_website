Getting Started

Quick Start Guide

Welcome to TrenchCalc! This guide will help you get started with your first trench calculations in minutes.

Step 1: Install the App

Download TrenchCalc from the Google Play Store and install it on your Android device.

Step 2: Create Your First Project

  1. Open TrenchCalc
  2. Tap the "+" button to create a new project
  3. Enter a project name (e.g., "Main Street Trench")

Step 3: Enter Trench Dimensions

Enter your trench dimensions:

  • Length: Total length of the trench
  • Depth: Depth from ground surface
  • Base Width: Width at the bottom
  • Top Width: Width at the surface (for trapezoidal trenches)
üí° Tip: You can switch between metric and imperial units using the settings menu.

System Requirements

  • Android 8.0 (API level 26) or higher

Your First Calculations

Basic Trench Volume

To calculate a simple rectangular trench volume:

  1. Open the app and create a new project
  2. Go to the "Dimensions" tab
  3. Enter:
    • Length: 100 meters
    • Depth: 2 meters
    • Base Width: 1.5 meters
  4. Tap "Calculate"
  5. View your results in the "Results" tab

Understanding Results

The results screen shows:

  • Volume: Total trench volume in cubic meters or cubic yards
  • Area: Cross-sectional area
  • Perimeter: Trench perimeter measurements
  • Material Required: Based on selected fill type

Multi-Zone Calculations

TrenchCalc allows you to define multiple zones within a single trench:

  • Foundation Zone: Base layer with specific material
  • Bed Zone: Main trench bed
  • Haunch Zone: Side support areas
  • Side Fill Zone: Backfill material
  • Overlay Zone: Surface layer
üí° Pro Tip: Use multi-zone calculations for complex projects with different materials in each zone. This gives you accurate volume and cost breakdowns.

Cement Stabilization

Overview

Cement stabilization is used to improve soil strength and durability. TrenchCalc helps you calculate the required cement content and predict strength gains.

Calculating Cement Requirements

  1. Navigate to the "Cement" tab
  2. Select your soil type (clay, sand, gravel, etc.)
  3. Enter target UCS (Unconfined Compressive Strength)
  4. Specify cement content percentage
  5. View calculated cement requirements

UCS Predictions

TrenchCalc predicts UCS based on:

  • Soil type and properties
  • Cement content percentage
  • Curing time (7-day and 28-day predictions)
  • Material-specific coefficients (ASTM standards)
Example: 5% cement content in clay soil ‚Üí 7-day UCS: ~0.5 MPa ‚Üí 28-day UCS: ~1.2 MPa

Mix Design

The app calculates:

  • Dry soil weight required
  • Cement weight needed
  • Water content for optimal compaction
  • Total stabilized mix volume

Photogrammetry

What is Photogrammetry?

Photogrammetry converts multiple photos into accurate 3D models. TrenchCalc uses this to create precise volume calculations from photos taken on-site.

Creating a Photogrammetry Scan

  1. Go to the "Site Survey" tab
  2. Tap "New Scan"
  3. Take 15-30 photos following the guidelines:
    • Overlap photos by 60-80%
    • Move around the trench in a circular pattern
    • Keep phone level and steady
    • Ensure good lighting
  4. Upload photos (processing happens in the cloud)
  5. Wait for processing (typically 5-10 minutes)
  6. View your 3D model and volume calculations
üí° Best Results: Take photos during good lighting conditions (avoid harsh shadows). Overlap photos significantly for better accuracy.

Photogrammetry Accuracy

TrenchCalc photogrammetry typically achieves:

  • ¬±1-3cm accuracy for volume measurements
  • DSM/DTM generation for terrain analysis
  • Point cloud visualization in 3D

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

App won't open or crashes

  • Force close the app and restart it
  • Clear app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí TrenchCalc ‚Üí Storage ‚Üí Clear Cache
  • Ensure you have the latest version from Play Store
  • Restart your device
  • Check available storage space

Calculations seem incorrect

  • Double-check all input values
  • Verify units are consistent (meters vs feet)
  • Check material density values
  • Review compaction factors
  • Export project and review all inputs

Cloud sync not working

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify you're signed in
  • Try signing out and back in
  • Check app permissions for network access

Photos not uploading

  • Check internet connection
  • Verify photo file size (try smaller photos)
  • Check available storage
  • Ensure app has photo permissions

Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate are the calculations?

TrenchCalc uses industry-standard formulas (ASTM, ACI) for all calculations. Volume calculations are mathematically precise based on your inputs. For photogrammetry, accuracy is typically ¬±1-3cm.

Can I use TrenchCalc offline?

Yes! Core calculations work completely offline. Cloud sync, photogrammetry, and AI features require internet connection.

What units are supported?

TrenchCalc supports both metric (meters, kilograms) and imperial (feet, pounds) units. You can switch between them in settings.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Subscriptions are managed through Google Play Store. Go to Play Store ‚Üí Subscriptions ‚Üí TrenchCalc ‚Üí Cancel. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Can I export my data?

Yes! You can export projects as PDF reports or CSV files. PDFs include all calculations and breakdowns. CSV files contain raw data for spreadsheet analysis.

What's the difference between Free, Basic, Premium, and MAX plans?

See the Pricing page for a complete comparison. Free includes basic calculations. Premium adds AI features, photogrammetry, and advanced planning. MAX includes everything with higher limits.
