Why TrenchCalc?

TrenchCalc goes far beyond basic volume calculations. It's a complete project planning tool designed for civil engineers, site supervisors, and contractors who need professional-grade calculations in the field.

Advanced Volume Calculations

Multi-zone trenching with support for vertical, battered, benched, and stepped walls. Calculate separate volumes for foundation, bed, haunch, side fill, and overlay zones with culvert integration.

Cement Stabilization Intelligence

Predict UCS (Unconfined Compressive Strength) for cement-stabilized materials. Get 7-day and 28-day strength projections with material-specific coefficients based on ASTM standards.

Quality Control Testing Planner

Generate automated field testing schedules based on volume and specifications. Plan compaction tests, PSD/Atterberg limits, and moisture content testing by lift and zone.

Professional Cost Estimation

Complete project pricing with material, labor, equipment, mobilization, overhead, and profit calculations. Export-ready estimates for tender submissions.

Duration & Scheduling

Calculate project timelines based on productivity rates. Get phase breakdowns for mobilization, construction, culvert installation, and crew planning with estimated finish dates.

Truckload & Logistics Planning

Plan material deliveries with truckload calculations based on capacity. Optimize delivery schedules for fill, cement, and stabilized mix materials.

Save & Load Projects

Store unlimited project configurations with named saves. Instantly recall complete trench setups and manage multiple projects simultaneously—no spreadsheet juggling.

Professional Export

Generate PDF and CSV reports with comprehensive material breakdowns, cost estimates, and testing schedules. Share directly from the app for project documentation.

Smart Material Management

6 preset materials with industry-standard properties. Assign different materials per zone with automatic density and compaction factor calculations for loose/compacted conversion.

What Sets TrenchCalc Apart

Engineering Intelligence: Not just formulas—understands civil engineering workflows and industry standards (ASTM, ACI, Austroads)
Complete Planning: Volume + Time + Cost + Quality + Logistics in one app
Multi-Zone Sophistication: 7 distinct zones with different materials, geometries, and cement stabilization per zone
Field-Ready: Works offline, fast input, visual aids, and safety warnings built-in
Professional Standards: Based on ASTM D6103, ACI 230.1R, and other recognized codes
Cross-Platform: Available on iOS and Android with cloud sync via AWS Cognito
