Frequently Asked Questions

How do I see pricing?

Pricing is shown in the app based on your region. Download TrenchCalc and tap on any premium feature to see subscription options in your local currency.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes! All subscriptions can be canceled at any time through Google Play. You'll keep access until the end of your billing period.

Is there a free trial?

The Free plan is available forever with core features. You can upgrade to Basic or Premium anytime to unlock advanced features.

What payment methods are accepted?

All payments are processed securely through Google Play, which accepts credit cards, PayPal, and carrier billing depending on your region.
